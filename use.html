Color Palette

The Curie AI primary color palette consists of Slate Blue, Teal, Light Blue, Aqua, Orange, and white. Slate Blue can be used as tints in even increments of 5% to create dividers and separation where needed.
Orange should primarily be used online as a CTA color. The Curie AI expanded color palette can be seen on page 3 of this document.
When using the Curie AI color palette, always choose a level of contrast that meets ADA accessibility requirements. Slate Blue text on white or light backgrounds, or white text on Slate Blue a background is preferred. Photography brings in additional color and warmth.
