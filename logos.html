Logos

The Curie AI logo lockup consists of a symbol and a logotype which together represent the idea of Augmented Intelligence. The logo lockup should be used across all communications in print and digital. For online instances such as LinkedIn, the symbol can be used as a standalone profile picture.
The full-color logo lockups come in a horizontal and vertical format for use on light and dark backgrounds. The logo lockups should always be used with sufficient background contrast. The horizontal logo lockup on a light background is the preferred brand expression.
